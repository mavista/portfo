Digital Voltmeter

Making use of logic gates and two PIC 16F84 micro-controllers, one as a transmitter and the other one as a receiver. Using a simplex serial link as means of communication between the two micro-controllers. The receiver drove three seven segment displays to display the voltage and the transmitter had external circuitry to aid it in analogue to digital conversion.

Microchip Integrated Development Environment

Demonstrate effective use of the Microchip Integrated Development Environment (IDE) in developing a Program on a PIC24FJ128GA microcontroller in C. The program had to measure room temperature using on-board sensors and display it on a 7 segment LED display with name and student number. The temperature had to be within 2 Degrees of current room temperature.
